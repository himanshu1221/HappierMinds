About Us

Curious ? Here is a brief about what we do



Happier minds aim to create a safe and interactive space for anyone and everyone who wants to talk. we try providing a lending ear . here we would love to hear any random stuff about you and your feelings. it's a place to make new friends , find yourself a buddy , a mentor or just discover the fact that you are understood! now you dont have to spend thousands on therapy or overwhelm yourselves with your thoughts.you can not just talk here with us but also become someone who listens by volunteering .
